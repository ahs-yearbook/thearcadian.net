# 2020-2021 Yearbook Staff ## Eics & Managing The editor-in-chiefs and managing editors head the entire yearbook process. They are in charge of making sure everything goes smoothly and put the final seal of approval before sending the proofs in to Balfour. As the leaders of this student-led publication, they oversee the editors, who in turn, watch over their respective sections. Deadlines, quality work, and creativity are all elements of The Arcadian that the EICs and managing editors are responsible for. Editor-in-Chiefs: Sarah Delima and Hannah Shin Managing Editors: Danielle Wong and Michelle Tan ## Academics Apaches hit the books hard, and it’s Academics section’s job to cover the plethora of classes and academic teams that keep our students busy. Other than the core curriculum classes, Academics covers nationally ranked teams, such as Constitution Team and Ocean Sciences Bowl. They also are responsible for seeking out everyday students taking a variety of classes to obtain an up-close-and-personal aspect to the AHS classroom.
